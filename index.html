About

I'm Shabih Ali, a UX Designer designer born in Patna, studying from Nift Mumbai. I am a trained Designer with a focused on research and identifying a problem through the UX process. I Iike being involved in innovation and human-centric design. My aim is to design products which users would loves to use product physically, mentally and emotionally.

Download my cv


What i do

User Experience

Conduct user research and testing. Developing wireframes and task flows based on user needs. Collaborate with Developers to create intuitive, user-friendly software.

Design Strategy

Understand the business needs of the customer and aligning the design and design process around those business needs. Each step of the design process and map to the goals and outcomes of the business problem that needs to be solved.

Interaction Design

Focus on creating engaging web interfaces with logical and thought out behaviors and actions. Successful interactive design uses technology and principles of good communication to create desired user experiences.
